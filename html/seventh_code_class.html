Solar System

In the solar system, there are 8 planets, and Earth is one of them.

For more images like this, visit N.A.S.A.

Our solar system has 8 planets in this order.

  1. Mercury
  2. Venus
  3. Earth
  4. Mars
  5. Jupiter
  6. Saturn
  7. Uranus
  8. Neptune

All these planets have few things in common.

  • They all have moons.
  • All of them have gas in them.
  • They have their own gravitational force.
  • None of them except Earth, has life.
